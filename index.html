About IDR

Building Tomorrow's Risk Leaders

The Institute for Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles. We prepare professionals for regulated, high-consequence environments where precision and expertise are paramount.

Through our unique partnership with leading UK universities, we bridge the gap between academic insight and practical application—ensuring our graduates are equipped with both theoretical foundations and hands-on experience in managing complex risk landscapes.
